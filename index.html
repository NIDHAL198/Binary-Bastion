About Binary Bastion

We are a dedicated group of Information Security Engineering students from Batna University, united by our passion for cybersecurity and cryptography. Our journey in the field has inspired us to create Binary Bastion, an interactive platform designed to simplify and demystify the complexities of modern cryptosystems.

As emerging professionals in the realm of information security, we believe in the power of hands-on learning and innovative technology to transform how cryptography is taught and understood.

Through this project, we aim not only to deepen our own knowledge but also to empower others by providing clear, engaging, and accessible content on critical security topics such as:

  • One-Time Pad (OTP)
  • Linear Feedback Shift Register (LFSR)
  • RSA Cryptosystem
  • Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC)
  • Diffie-Hellman Key Exchange
  • ElGamal Encryption

We are committed to building secure, user-friendly applications and continuously exploring new ways to bring advanced cryptographic concepts to life.
